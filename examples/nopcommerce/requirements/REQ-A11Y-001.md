# REQ-A11Y-001: Storefront home-page accessibility

## Business purpose

The store home page shall not contain critical or serious automated accessibility violations within the selected WCAG rules, so common barriers are detected before they reach shoppers.

## Scope

- The anonymously accessible nopCommerce home page.
- Automated rules tagged for WCAG 2.0 and WCAG 2.1 levels A and AA.
- The rendered page state after its meaningful heading and title are ready.

## Preconditions

- The local storefront is installed with sample data and responds successfully.
- The test runs in a fresh Chromium browser context.

## Acceptance criteria

### AC-A11Y-001

The home page reaches its defined business-ready state before scanning begins.

### AC-A11Y-002

The evaluated page contains no critical automated accessibility violations.

### AC-A11Y-003

The evaluated page contains no serious automated accessibility violations.

### AC-A11Y-004

Moderate and minor findings remain visible in sanitized Playwright evidence without causing the configured test to fail.

### AC-A11Y-005

The scan retains bounded rule, impact, help, target, and failure-summary evidence without retaining full HTML, form values, cookies, headers, or credentials.

## Automated-check limitations

One automated scan does not prove complete WCAG conformance. This requirement covers only the selected page state and configured automated rules. Keyboard navigation, screen-reader behavior, focus order, zoom and reflow, cognitive usability, meaningful content, and other assistive-technology experiences still require manual and broader testing.

## Risk

High. Critical or serious barriers can prevent shoppers with disabilities from understanding or operating the storefront.

## Out of scope

- Checkout, account, administrator, and other storefront pages
- Manual keyboard and screen-reader assessment
- Cross-browser accessibility comparison
- Visual regression and accessibility conformance certification
- Anonymous or undocumented rule exclusions
